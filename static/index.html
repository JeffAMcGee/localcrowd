About VineMap

This is a map of Vines that are publicly posted to Twitter updated in real-time. The location of a vine on the map is based on the location field in a user's profile. Vines by people without a public location, retweets, and Vines Twitter has marked as sensitive have been removed, but some objectionable content may still get through. This demo has no affiliation with Twitter or Vine.
